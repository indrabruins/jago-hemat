FAQ

Got Questions?

Is the food safe to eat?

Absolutely yes. Every item listed on Jago Hemat is still within its safe consumption period — verified by our quality team. All food is clearly labeled with expiry dates, and our partner stores are vetted before joining. We follow Indonesian food safety regulations (BPOM) strictly.

How does pickup work?

After reserving your bag through the app, you'll receive a confirmation with the pickup window and store address. Simply show the confirmation at the store and collect your food.

How much can I save?

Our average user saves Rp 340,000 per month. If you use Jago Hemat regularly, it's not unusual to save Rp 1-2 million per month on groceries and dining.

What if I'm not satisfied?

We have a 30-day happiness guarantee. If you're not satisfied with your experience, contact us via WhatsApp and we'll issue a full refund — no questions asked.

How can stores join?

Register your store in 5 minutes using the form above. We'll verify your business, set up your listing, and you can start selling surplus within 48 hours.

Is Jago Hemat available in my city?

We're currently live in Jakarta, Surabaya, Bandung, Bali, and Semarang. We're expanding rapidly.

Are there any hidden fees?

Zero. Jago Hemat is completely free for users to download and use.

Do you deliver or is it pickup only?

Currently Jago Hemat is pickup-only — you collect your bag from the store.
