## 3. LIGHT THEME (PRIMARY — Indonesian-First)

### Concept: "Sunny & Appetizing"
Light theme is the HERO theme — it matches the app and the existing website. Food-forward, warm, fresh, appetizing.

**Color Palette:**
```
--bg: #FFD740          ← THE signature yellow (curved/wave background)
--card: #FFFFFF        ← White cards on yellow
--surface: #FFF8E1     ← Warm cream for secondary surfaces
--accent: #2E7D32      ← Forest green (official brand)
--accent-light: #4CAF50 ← Bright green (hover states)
--text: #212121        ← Near black
--text-muted: #757575  ← Gray
--border: rgba(0,0,0,0.08)
--cta: #2E7D32         ← Green CTA buttons
--cta-text: #FFFFFF    ← White text on green
```

**Signature Element:** The curved yellow background — exactly like jagohemat.com. Large SVG wave/curve at top of page, flowing yellow throughout the hero, with white cards overlaid.

**Food Photography:** Real food images throughout — colorful, appetizing, Indonesian food. Use high-quality Unsplash images of: nasi box, roti, kue, vegetables, fruits, Indonesian dishes. NO abstract illustrations.

## 4. DARK THEME (SECONDARY)

### Concept: "Premium Mission"
Dark mode is for contrast and variety — feels like a premium food app at night. Dark charcoal background, same emerald green accents, food photography remains prominent.

**Color Palette:**
```
--bg: #0D0D0F          ← Near-black
--card: #1A1A1E        ← Dark card
--surface: #252529     ← Elevated surface
--accent: #2E7D32      ← Forest green (same official brand)
--accent-light: #4CAF50 ← Bright green
--warning: #FFC107      ← Golden yellow (for urgency)
--text: #FFFFFF        ← White
--text-muted: #9CA3AF  ← Muted gray
--border: rgba(255,255,255,0.08)
```

**Dark Theme Approach:**
- Same sections as light, same layout
- Dark backgrounds throughout
- Same emerald green (#2E7D32) accent color
- Food photography still visible (images on dark cards work well)
- The contrast between dark + green creates a premium feel
- Scroll reveal animations same as light
