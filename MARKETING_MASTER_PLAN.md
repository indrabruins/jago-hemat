# JAGO HEMAT — MARKETING MASTER PLAN
## Jakarta Launch Edition | v1.0 | April 1, 2026

---

## EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

**Objective:** Build a two-sided marketplace in Jakarta — merchants who list surplus food, users who buy it — with a sustainable unit economics model.

**Single most important insight:** Merchants come FIRST. Supply creates its own demand in the food space. Every other food app got this backwards and paid for it. With Jago Hemat, the growth engine is merchant density → user value → viral coefficient → more users → more merchant revenue. Get the supply right and users self-generate.

**Competitive position:** No direct competitor in Indonesia. Too Good To Go is not in Southeast Asia. Surplus Indonesia exists but has weak merchant onboarding (no WhatsApp-first), dated UX, and limited ESG product. **The window is open.**

## SECTION 1: STRATEGIC FRAMEWORK

### Growth Sequence
```
Week 1-4: MERCHANT SUPPLY FIRST (no users until supply is ready)
Week 3-6: Soft-launch users with anchor merchants
Week 6-12: Aggressive dual-sided growth
Month 3-6: Scale and expand coverage
```

### The Flywheel
```
Merchants list surplus → Users discover good deals → Users save money + feel good →
Users tell friends (referral) → More users → More revenue per merchant →
More merchants join → More supply → Better discovery → Repeat
```

### Commission Model
| Phase | Commission | Rationale |
|---|---|---|
| Year 1 (MVP) | **0%** | Build supply, prove unit economics, beat competitors on trust |
| Year 2 | **10%** | Sustainable margin, still below TGTG's 20-30% |
| Year 3+ | **15%** | Platform takes value (ESG reports, analytics, delivery) |

> **Flashfood benchmark:** 5% commission or max $2.99/order. Don't race to the bottom on commission — race to the top on trust and merchant experience.

**What to bring:**

### The Referral Mechanic: "Teman Hemat, 3x Gratis"

**The mechanic:**
```
User A shares unique link → sends to WhatsApp group
User B clicks link + completes first pickup
User A gets: 1 stamp (of 3 needed for free bundle)
User B gets: Rp 10-15K credit on first pickup

Collect 3 stamps → unlocks "Paket Hemat 3x" = 3 free pickups (any restaurant, up to Rp 20K value each)
```

**Why it works in Jakarta WhatsApp groups:**
- Personal link ("Dari Sarah, Jakarta Selatan") gets opened 3-5x more than brand links
- "Gratis" is culturally irresistible — free pickup for referee, stamps for referrer
- WhatsApp "forwarded many times" label signals popularity → more clicks
- Eco-identity makes sharing feel generous, not spammy

## SECTION 7: CRITICAL RISKS & MITIGATIONS

### 🚨 CRITICAL: BPOM Regulatory Risk
**Risk:** BPOM Regulation No. 26/2022 — commercial establishments may be prohibited from *reselling repacked food* without NIE (marketing authorization).

**Mitigation:**
1. Frame all listings as "pre-order/pickup of remaining menu items" NOT packaged food products
2. Get formal legal opinion from Jakarta food law firm IMMEDIATELY
3. Register as a technology platform (PT), not a food business — merchant bears food safety liability
4. Build food safety dispute resolution mechanism into the app
5. Consider BPOM pilot partnership for legitimacy

### 🚨 Risk: Merchant Supply Stalls
**Risk:** Merchants sign up but don't list consistently.
**Mitigation:** Daily WhatsApp check-in SOP. ROI reminder messages ("Kemarin: 8kg food kamu tidak laku. Hari ini?").

### 🚨 Risk: Unit Economics Unsustainable
**Risk:** 0% commission + operational costs = negative margin.
**Mitigation:** Track contribution margin per order from Day 1. Set commission switch date (Month 9-12) based on data, not a calendar.

### 🚨 Risk: User Expectations vs. Reality
**Risk:** "Surprise bag" content varies → disappointment → bad reviews.
**Mitigation:** Show photo of actual food where possible. "Value guarantee" — if bag is clearly below stated value, refund.
