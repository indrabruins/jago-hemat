# Jago Hemat — Marketing Improvement Playbook
**Status:** ACTIVE | **CEO:** Kit | **Date:** 2026-03-29

## What Jago Hemat Is
Indonesian food waste/surplus grocery marketplace — users buy 50-80% off near-expiry food from partner stores. Available on Play Store + App Store.

---

## Competitive Landscape — What's Working

### Too Good To Go (global leader)
- **Main growth driver:** Organic UGC — 160K+ Instagram posts, 80K+ TikTok posts with #TooGoodToGo
- **Merchant value prop:** Not just "clear surplus" — it's "attract new clientele." Customers who try discounted Surprise Bags often come back for full-price purchases
- **Key stat they sell to merchants:** One grocery partner → 12,280 meals saved, $47,659 in revenue, 87% customer return rate
- **Brand shift 2026:** From niche/waste-focused → top-of-mind "default choice" for food purchasing

### Flashfood (North America)
- **Zone model:** Geo-density first — consolidate coverage in one area before expanding, creating network effects
- **ML personalization:** Push notifications for deals within 5-mile radius based on purchase history
- **Scarcity at scale:** 75%+ sell-through on listed items
- **Retailer co-marketing:** "Rethink Your Shrink" — positioned as revenue tool, not discount bin
- **In-app gamification:** New users, flash sales, bulk incentives. 65% more store visits from app users
- **SNAP EBT integration:** Expanded addressable market

### Olio (community model)
- **60% of new sign-ups via word-of-mouth**
- **50,000 volunteer ambassadors** — distribute flyers, post on WhatsApp, local grassroots activation
- **30-minute listing requests** for food → rapid virality
- **Impact tracking:** Users see "meals rescued, carbon saved" → emotional retention

### Indonesia-Specific Context
- 190M+ smartphone users — mobile-first everything
- GrabMart, GoFood dominate — super-app ecosystem cross-promotion is massive
- Bahasa Indonesia, halal positioning, regional pricing (Java vs. Sulawesi)
- Merchant onboarding: 5-minute KTP registration is the standard expectation
- QRIS payment integration essential
- Micro-influencers + TikTok live commerce = highest ROI for grocery

## TOP 10 MARKETING HOOKS FOR JAGO HEMAT

### For Users (Consumer Hooks)

**1. The FOMO Hunger Hook**
> *"Indonesia wastes 20.93 million tons of food per year. 44% is still perfectly good to eat. You're leaving free food on the table."*
- Works because: it reframes the app as solving an injustice, not just a deal

**2. The Math Hook**
> *"The average Indonesian family throws away Rp 3.4M/year in food. Jago Hemat recovers Rp 1.7M of that."*
- Works because: concrete local numbers hit harder than percentages

**3. The Urgency Hook**
> *"3 bags left from this bakery — pickup expires in 2 hours"*
- Works because: real-time scarcity + FOMO drives immediate action (15-25% conversion lift)

**4. The Planet Hook**
> *"Every Rp 50,000 spent = 2kg CO₂ saved. Your grocery run fights climate change."*
- Works because: environmental identity resonates with urban Indonesian millennials/gen-Z

**5. The Surprise Box Hook (Too Good To Go's playbook)**
> *"You never know what you'll get — that's the fun. Restaurants pack up their excess, you pay 50% less."*
- Works because: gamification + mystery = social sharing

**6. The "Neighbors Are Doing It" Hook**
> *"12 people in your area claimed a Jago Hemat deal today."*
- Works because: real social proof in real-time (highest conversion when combined with scarcity)

### For Merchants (Business Hooks)

**7. The "Rethink Your Shrink" Hook**
> *"Your end-of-day markdown strategy is costing you margin. Jago Hemat turns your surplus into repeat customers."*
- Works because: positions app as revenue tool, not discount channel

**8. The ROI Proof Hook**
> *"Merchants on Jago Hemat recover Rp 8-15M/month in otherwise-wasted inventory. Average customer return rate: 78%."*
- Works because: specific, local currency, proof-based

**9. The "New Clientele" Hook**
> *"67% of Jago Hemat users visit their store again for full-price purchases after their first pickup."*
- Works because: merchants care about customer lifetime value, not just waste reduction

**10. The "5-Minute Setup" Hook**
> *"List your first surplus item in 5 minutes. No paperwork. No commission on your first 30 days."*
- Works because: removes friction from merchant onboarding

## MERCHANT ACQUISITION STRATEGY

### Phase 1 — Anchor Stores (Weeks 1-4)
1. Target **3-5 high-volume outlets** per pilot neighborhood (bakery chains, minimart franchises,的水果 shops)
2. Offer: **zero commission for first 30 days** + featured placement on app home screen
3. Provide: printed QR code cards for checkout counter display
4. Deliver: weekly WhatsApp report showing meals saved + revenue recovered

### Phase 2 — Neighborhood Expansion (Weeks 5-12)
1. **Zone-based:** Go deep in 1-2 Jakarta neighborhoods before expanding
2. **Micro-influencer activation:** Partner with 5-10 local food bloggers per zone
3. ** ambassador program:** Olio-style — recruit store owners as ambassadors, give referral commission

### Phase 3 — Chain & Franchise (Month 4+)
1. Approach **franchise brands** (Chatime, BreadTalk, Ranch Market) for enterprise rollout
2. "Rethink Your Shrink" B2B landing page with ROI calculator
3. LinkedIn/FB ads targeting retail operations decision-makers

### Merchant Onboarding (Make it 5 minutes)
- KTP photo upload → AI extracts business details
- Store photos → auto-cropped and uploaded
- First listing → templated with one-tap pricing suggestions
- WhatsApp alert when someone buys → merchant confirms pickup window

## USER ACQUISITION STRATEGY

### Primary Channels (Indonesia mobile-first)

**TikTok (highest ROI)**
- "Haul" content: influencer picks up Jago Hemat bag → shows what's inside → reaction
- "Waste comparison" content: what restaurants throw away vs. what you get
- Behind-the-scenes: how the surplus-to-deal pipeline works
- Budget: 60% of paid social spend

**Instagram / Reels**
- User-generated content program: incentivize posts with #JagoHemat
- "What I got in my bag" weekly features
- Merchant spotlight: "Meet the bakery saving 200 meals/week"
- Budget: 25% of paid social

**WhatsApp (the Indonesian social layer)**
- Shareable deal cards: image + price + "Claim now" link
- Neighborhood groups: partner with RW/Kelurahan admins
- Broadcast list for flash sale alerts (opt-in)

**Referral Program**
- Give Rp 10,000 credit for each friend who makes first purchase
- Merchant referrals: Rp 25,000 credit when a merchant you refer lists for 7 days

### Retention Mechanics
- **Impact dashboard:** "You've saved 47 meals, Rp 235,000, and 94kg CO₂"
- **Streak rewards:** "Pick up 4 weeks in a row = free pickup from any merchant"
- **Surprise bonus bags:** Random "mystery bag" upgrades for loyal users

## WEBSITE IMPROVEMENTS (Priority Order)

### 1. ABOVE THE FOLD (Highest Impact)
- Hero: Replace vague copy → **"Makanan Enak, Harga Hemat. Selamatkan 50% dari Pemborosan."**
- Add: **"Download App"** buttons (Google Play + App Store) — currently missing or buried
- Add: **"X items available near you right now"** — real-time, geo-specific
- Add: **Impact counter** — "13,402 meals saved this month" (animated count-up)

### 2. TRUST / SAFETY (Top objection handler)
- FAQ card: **"Is the food safe?"** → answer with regulatory + food safety explanation
- Badge: **"100% aman untuk dikonsumsi"** (100% safe to consume)
- Photo proof: Side-by-side of store shelf vs. Jago Hemat bag contents

### 3. URGENCY / SCARCITY
- **"Hampir Habis" (Almost Gone)** section — live inventory count
- Countdown timer on flash deals
- "X more bags from this store today" in listing previews

### 4. SOCIAL PROOF
- **User testimonials** with names, photos, location (Jaksel, Bandung, Surabaya)
- **Merchant testimonials** — especially small business owners
- **Real numbers:** 50K+ users, 500+ stores, Rp 2B+ saved for users

### 5. STORE LOCATOR (Geo-relevance)
- **"Find stores near you"** — ZIP/kelurahan input above the fold
- Map integration with live inventory levels per store
- "Most popular this week" badge on high-volume stores

### 6. MERCHANT CTA
- Dedicated **"For Business Owners"** section
- Simple 3-step "Start Selling" flow
- ROI calculator: "See how much you're currently losing to waste → calculate your potential"

### 7. AMBASSADOR PROGRAM (Olio model)
- "Join the Jago Hemat Squad" — volunteer ambassador sign-up
- WhatsApp community for ambassadors
- Referral codes + commission

### 8. IMPACT TRACKING
- **Live counter:** meals saved, CO₂ reduced, money saved
- Personal impact for logged-in users
- Shareable impact card → for WhatsApp/TikTok
